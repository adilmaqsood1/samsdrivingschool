What is the Driver’s License History?

The Driver’s License History provides proof of successful completion and graduation from a BDE course and is widely recognized by the insurance industry as proof of course completion. BDE graduates with a Driver’s License History may be eligible for an insurance discount.

Can I take my G2 road test in 8 months?

Yes. If you successfully complete a Driver Beginner Education (BDE) course, you may be eligible to take your G2 road test sooner.

Can I start my driver training course immediately?

Yes. You can start right away, and you may even begin in-car lessons (with your G1) before completing the in-class sessions.

Can I start the course without a G1 license yet?

Yes. We can help you prepare to pass the knowledge/written test for the Driver’s Examination Centre.

Can I drive with my G2 on the highway?

Yes. You can drive on the highway anytime, anywhere, as long as you follow the rules and restrictions that apply to your licence.
